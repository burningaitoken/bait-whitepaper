Executive Summary

Burning AI Token (BAIT) represents a groundbreaking convergence of artificial intelligence and deflationary tokenomics on the Binance Smart Chain. As a BEP-20 token with a maximum supply of 100 million tokens, BAIT implements an automated burning mechanism that permanently removes 1.5% of the remaining supply annually, creating a sustainable deflationary model that accelerates scarcity over time.

Core Innovation: BAIT integrates AI-driven analytics with a transparent burning protocol to create a self-regulating economic system where scarcity increases predictably, driving organic value appreciation while maintaining ecosystem sustainability.

The project's ultimate goal is to reach a final circulating supply of only 10 million BAIT tokens, representing a 90% reduction from the initial supply, making each remaining token increasingly valuable as the burning mechanism progresses through its scheduled phases.
